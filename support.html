Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Open the Dinner Roll app and tap "Sign Up". You can create an account using your email address and a password, or sign in with Google.

What's the difference between Plus and Pro?

  • Plus ($2.99/month): Removes ads and allows recipe rating
  • Pro ($4.99/month): Everything in Plus, plus unlimited recipe saves, chef following, and social media links

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time:

  • Android: Open Google Play Store ‚Üí Subscriptions ‚Üí Select Dinner Roll ‚Üí Cancel
  • iOS: Open Settings ‚Üí Apple ID ‚Üí Subscriptions ‚Üí Select Dinner Roll ‚Üí Cancel Subscription

You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.

How do I restore my purchases?

If you've previously purchased a subscription on another device or after reinstalling the app:

  1. Open the Dinner Roll app
  2. Go to the Subscription screen
  3. Tap "Restore Purchases"

Your subscription will be restored if it's still active.

Can I save recipes on the free plan?

Yes! The free (Basic) plan allows you to save up to 10 recipes. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited saves.

Why can't I follow chefs?

Following chefs is a Pro feature. Upgrade to Pro to follow your favorite chefs and get notified about their new recipes.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account:

  1. Open the Dinner Roll app
  2. Go to Profile ‚Üí Settings
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm the deletion

Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

I'm having trouble with payments

Subscription payments are processed through Google Play (Android) or the Apple App Store (iOS). For payment issues, please contact:

  • Android: Google Play Support
  • iOS: Apple App Store Support

How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

We'd love to hear from you! Email us at support@dinnerroll.app with details about the issue or your feature suggestion.
